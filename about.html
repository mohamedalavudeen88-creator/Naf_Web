Our Journey

A Decade of Excellence & Growth

From a small security company in Chennai to a comprehensive facility services leader across Tamil Nadu.

2014
Founded in Chennai

NAF Security Services established with a vision to provide professional, reliable security solutions to Chennai's growing corporate sector.

2015
PSARA License Obtained

Secured PSARA license and expanded to serve 15+ corporate clients in the IT corridor of Taramani and Old Mahabalipuram Road.

2017
Facility Services Launched

Expanded service portfolio to include housekeeping, facility management, and manpower supply — becoming a full-service FM company.

2019
Triple ISO Certified

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management), ISO 14001 (Environmental Management), and OHSAS 18001 TUV NORD (Occupational Health & Safety) certifications — setting a new standard for compliance in the FM sector.

2021
Technology Integration

Introduced GPS guard tracking, biometric attendance, and digital reporting systems — setting new standards for transparency and accountability.

2024
100+ Clients, 25+ Locations

Crossed 100+ active client engagements across Chennai, Coimbatore & Madurai — serving hospitals, IT parks, factories and residential complexes.

Core Values

The Principles We Work By

At NAF, we have chosen to operate through a set of shared values. These values are fundamental to everything we do and adhering to them is the only way to ensure that we run a sustainable business.

Collaborate

We believe in the power of teamwork — partnering with clients, employees and communities to create environments that thrive through shared effort and mutual respect.

Excellence

We consistently strive to exceed expectations — training our staff continuously, refining our processes, and setting higher benchmarks for service delivery every year.

Respect

We treat every person — client, staff, contractor or visitor — with dignity and respect. We believe a respectful culture enables excellence and drives long-term loyalty.

Integrity

We operate with complete honesty and transparency in every contract, every deployment, and every client interaction — no compromise, ever.

Sustainability

By aligning with our customers' goals of enhancing site operations while reducing energy consumption, we develop long-term, affordable sustainability strategies for every facility.

Client Focus

Everything we do is focused on our clients. We are all about service excellence and measure our progress by client satisfaction. Our FM contract retention stands above 90%.
