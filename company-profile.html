Our Journey

35+ Years of Milestones

From a single recruitment office in India to a multi-national integrated FM group — every year has added a new chapter.

1988

Al Najma Al Fareeda Group founded by Jamal Mohammad in India as a recruitment consultancy company. Al Najma Al Fareeda Super Travels also started.

1996

Gulf operations launched in Kuwait. Al Najma Al Fareeda International Employment Agency commenced operations in Kuwait.

2006

UAE operations launched as an international cleaning company. Multiple subsidiary companies established across UAE to realise the "One Stop Shop Facilities Management Services" vision.

2010

NAF Facilities Management Services formally established as a dedicated FM brand within the group.

2014

Al Najma Al Fareeda International Employment Agency expanded to UAE, growing the group's cross-border workforce solutions.

2015

Al Najma Al Fareeda Hypermarket launched in India, diversifying the group into retail.

2016

Rope Access & Water Tank Cleaning services introduced in UAE — expanding the group's specialist technical capabilities.

2017

NAF Facilities Management Services India launched — bringing the group's proven FM model to Tamil Nadu. Al Najma Al Fareeda International Security also established in India.

2018

Security Services launched in Kuwait, extending the group's security arm to the Gulf market.

2019

NAF Facilities Management Services launched in Kuwait. Contracts shift from small, locally-procured engagements to long-term regional and national integrated FM packages — a transformational growth milestone.

2020

Disinfection & Sanitization Company launched against COVID-19 cases — approved by Dubai Municipality. Al Najma Al Fareeda International Employment Consultancy UAE also established.

2021

Total Facilities Management Services introduced in UAE — consolidating all FM subsidiaries under a single integrated service delivery model.

2022

Taqdeer Award — Recognised for Labour Excellence by the UAE Ministry of Labour. One of the most prestigious employer accolades in the Middle East.

2024

Al Najma Al Fareeda Group launches "Home Care Solutions" — Your Home Maintenance People. A new consumer-facing brand bringing the group's expertise directly to residential households.
